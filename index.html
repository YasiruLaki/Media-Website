Richmond Crowned the Runners Up

Richmond College crowned the runners up of the SLSCA Under 19 Division 1 Tier A Limited Over Cricket Tournament. Our warmest congradulations are convayed to the victorious Richmond Cricketers.

Lovers Quarrel 2022
Lovers Quarrel Ends in Draw

The 117th Richmond - Mahinda Lovers Quarrel was held on the days of 17th and 18th April. The iconic match came to an early end on the 2nd day due to bad weather conditions resulting in a draw.
